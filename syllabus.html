B.Com CA Syllabus Overview

The B.Com CA syllabus covers various aspects of commerce, finance, and business management. Below are the main subjects for each semester:

Semester 1

  • Principles of Management
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Communication

Semester 2

  • Cost Accounting
  • Business Law
  • Economics for Managers

Semester 3

  • Corporate Accounting
  • Taxation
  • Marketing Management
